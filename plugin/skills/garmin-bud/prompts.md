# GarminBud example prompts

Use after setup (`/garmin-bud:garmin-bud-setup` + restart).

## Single-topic

- What did I do today?
- Show me all my runs from last week.
- How's my sleep been this week?
- Is my resting heart rate trending better?
- Am I recovered enough to train hard today?
- How stressed have I been this week?
- What's my VO2 max trend over the last month?

## Compound (best results)

- I played tennis yesterday — how was my sleep after, and am I recovered today?
- Give me a weekly training summary: activities, sleep average, recovery trend.
- I'm planning a hard run tomorrow — based on sleep and recovery, is that a good idea?
- Am I overtraining? Look at my activities, sleep, stress, and recovery together.
- Summarize my fitness this week in plain English — workouts, sleep, HR, and weight.
